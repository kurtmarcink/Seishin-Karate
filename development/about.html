Quality Instruction For the Whole Family

Seishin has been an integral part of the Greater Boston community since 2000. We help students of all ages become great athletes, and better people.

Dedication

At Seishin, we understand the value of hard work. Our background is in Kenpo, a practical martial art that enhances power and coordination. We are also one of the leading training centers in the nation for Sport Karate, a modern martial art that sees tremendous popularity in Hollywood. Our training sessions enforce strength, flexibility, and stamina through high-energy cardio, calisthenics, and plyometrics.

Community

Seishin is not just a karate school, it's a way to meet great people and make friends for life. Students and parents enjoy our many events throughout the year, including movie nights, holiday parties, tournaments, demonstrations, and charity events.

Students at Seishin love giving back. We have strong ties with local non-profit organizations, including The Ryan Harvey Foundation, Medway Public Library, and Pine Hill Community School Association.

Success

Our success speaks for itself. Seishin has produced nearly 20 State Champions, 14 National Champions, and Four World Champions. Eleven members of Seishin have gone on to join the elite "Team Straight Up", a world-renowned professional competition team. Students have been featured in documentaries, music videos, commercials, and on ESPN.

Our instructors also has extensive experience teaching students with ADD, ADHD, Dyslexia, Learning Delay, Autism, Down Syndrome, and Cerebral Palsy, as well as those with physical limitations
